About Tomas

Hi, Tomas here. I'm a music producer, composer, sound engineer and educator based in Leeds, UK and I specialise in digital electronic music.

I create online music production courses on Udemy, Skillshare and Amazon and I am also the admin for the Electra Music Production Facebook Group.

I've worked with the likes of Peter Darling, Paul Statham, Paul Baily, Lizzie Joyce, Sean Genockey and Gav Venture.

I've released the 'Focal' EP under my own name is 2016 and 'Shoot the Demons off My Back' EP in 2015 and my latest Album 'Skylight' will be released in early 2017.

I've recently published an online video course called 'Music Production in Logic Pro X - The Complete Course and also 'Music Production in Ableton Live 9 - The Complete Course' with Udemy and I'm now working on a Music Theory Video course, audiobook, ebook and printed book due to be released in mid 2017.
